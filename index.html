1. Introduction

The current design of economy-class seats in low-cost carriers (LCCs) falls short in addressing passenger comfort, especially on long-haul flights. Limited legroom, constrained seating posture, and lack of adjustability for diverse seating preferences contribute to common discomforts such as fatigue, stiffness, poor posture, and leg heaviness. This project seeks to create an innovative aircraft seat design that enhances comfort within the constraints of LCCs, aiming to improve passenger experience and offer LCCs a new competitive advantage beyond pricing.
